Heads-up — the long day

Before you drive

The handful of things that make or break this specific route.

The car

Skip the cheapest economy box. A Golf, Octavia or Focus-class car makes the mountain roads a joy, not a chore — and clears the Măgura access road comfortably.

Book ahead

Peleș (timed tickets, closed Mon/Tue), Bran (slot to skip queues), and the Libearty bear sanctuary (morning slots only). Do these online the day before.

Mountain road hours

Transfăgărășan: 07:00–21:00 daily. Transalpina high section: shut 20:00–09:00 through 30 June, with a 30 km/h limit on the reopened stretch. Plan starts around them.

Fuel discipline

Top up before both scenic roads — at Curtea de Argeș / Vidraru for the Transfăgărășan, and at Sebeș for the Transalpina. No reliable stations up top.

Drive in shifts

Sunday and Monday are back-to-back alpine days. Let whoever isn't driving the scenic stretch take the transit legs. Rotate; stay fresh.

Bears & weather

This is Europe's densest bear range — never stop to approach or feed them. At 2,000 m the weather turns in minutes: pack layers and a rain shell even in June.
